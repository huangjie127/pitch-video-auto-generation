# Brand archetypes — anchor recipes by brand type

The restraint anchor (see `shot-prompt-language.md`) is the base. This file
maps common brand types to the *variable* parts: world (light/dark), texture
and lighting words, palette policy, tempo, music brief, and the metaphor
vocabulary that tends to land. Identify the archetype from the page screenshot
+ copy tone, start from the closest recipe, then adjust to what you actually
saw. These are starting points, not cages — two archetypes can blend (a
luxury dev-tool exists).

Field-tested: ① dev-infra (Atlas Cloud, dark painterly glass) and ② palette-
identity creative tool (Figma, light paper acrylic). The rest extrapolate the
same logic — steal their structure, verify against the brand's real site.

---

## ① Dev infra / cloud / AI platform (field-tested)

- **World**: deep black void. **Texture/light**: liquid glass, holographic
  crystal, chiaroscuro, one soft directional beam; optionally painterly
  (impasto grain) if the brand uses artful visuals.
- **Palette**: single accent = brand color; everything else neutral glass.
- **Tempo**: stately 5s beats. **Music**: cold precise ambient electronic,
  85–95bpm, evolving pads, sub bass.
- **Metaphors**: fragments→one object (unification), light filament racing
  through channels (speed), lattice reveal (scale), monolith pulse (uptime),
  edge-lit black glass signature close.

## ② Creative tools with palette identity (field-tested)

- **World**: warm white paper, subtle grain. **Texture/light**: glossy
  acrylic / clear glass objects, flat crisp geometry, soft daylight, precise
  shadows.
- **Palette**: ONLY the brand's palette on hero objects (name the hexes),
  everything else white/ink/clear. Closing beat may assemble the objects into
  a logo-reminiscent arrangement — the strongest memory hook.
- **Tempo**: silky-fast 4s beats, snappy easing with soft landings.
  **Music**: energetic minimal electronic, bright plucks, 115–125bpm.
- **Metaphors**: shapes escaping frames (freedom), snap-to-alignment with
  guide lines (the tool's magic), stacking layers (building), pointer
  triangles converging (collaboration), grid of canvases (scale).

## ③ Consumer hardware (phones, audio, wearables)

- **World**: dark studio, product-photography language. **Texture/light**:
  brushed metal, matte ceramic, sapphire glass, macro material close-ups,
  rim lighting, soft gradient falloff on black.
- **Palette**: the device's own finish + one brand accent for light effects.
- **Tempo**: 4–5s mixed; macro-slow on materials, brisk on transitions.
  **Music**: warm cinematic electronic with a heartbeat pulse, 95–110bpm.
- **Metaphors**: the object assembling from precision parts, light tracing a
  chamfered edge, fabric/liquid reacting to sound (audio), the device
  rotating into signature silhouette. NOTE: without real product renders the
  model invents a fictional device — keep it abstract (edges, materials,
  silhouettes) or feed real shots via image refs.

## ④ Luxury / premium lifestyle (watches, fragrance, auto)

- **World**: near-black with rich warmth. **Texture/light**: chiaroscuro,
  silk, polished obsidian, gold or amber glints, slow dust motes in a beam.
- **Palette**: black + one precious tone (gold/champagne/oxblood).
- **Tempo**: the slowest — 5s beats, one motion per shot, long stillness.
  **Music**: sparse piano or strings over deep ambience, 70–85bpm.
- **Metaphors**: mechanisms meshing (craft), liquid gold forming the object,
  a single beam revealing form out of darkness, the signature close held
  extra long. Copy: fewer, shorter lines; the film may hold 2 beats with no
  text at all.

## ⑤ Fintech / security / enterprise trust

- **World**: deep navy-to-black, architectural. **Texture/light**: precise
  geometric volumes, brushed steel and glass, grid floors, cool even light.
- **Palette**: one trust tone (deep blue, emerald) + white.
- **Tempo**: measured 4–5s, motion is *engineered* (linear glides, exact
  stops), never playful. **Music**: confident pulse, low strings + electronic,
  90–100bpm.
- **Metaphors**: vault door precision (security), beams joining into a
  bridge (connection), a ledger of light lines balancing (accuracy), shield
  forming from particles that lock — not float — into place.

## ⑥ Health / wellness / lifestyle apps

- **World**: bright airy daylight, warm neutrals. **Texture/light**: frosted
  glass, soft organic curves, morning-light shadows, gentle depth of field.
- **Palette**: one calm accent (sage, sky, coral) on warm white.
- **Tempo**: relaxed 4–5s, motion breathes (ease-in-out swells). **Music**:
  organic electronic with real instruments (piano, soft guitar), 90–100bpm.
- **Metaphors**: a form inhaling/exhaling (breath), scattered droplets
  merging calmly (balance), light passing through frosted layers (clarity),
  a curve straightening (progress).

## ⑦ Games / entertainment / social

- **World**: dark but SATURATED — energy allowed. **Texture/light**: neon
  edge light, volumetric haze, dramatic contrast; this is the one archetype
  where more energy beats restraint (still: one idea per shot).
- **Palette**: 2 electric tones max (e.g. magenta + cyan), on black.
- **Tempo**: fastest — all 4s, hard motion accents timed to beats. **Music**:
  driving electronic/hybrid trailer, 120–140bpm.
- **Metaphors**: portal opening, object charging up then releasing, camera
  whip-reveals (the exception to "no whip pans" — ONE, at the reveal),
  particle burst on the name card (the exception to "no particles" — once).

## ⑧ B2B SaaS / productivity

- **World**: light or dual-tone (light film, dark closing card). **Texture**:
  clean glass panels, paper, subtle depth — closest to archetype ② but with a
  single accent and calmer tempo.
- **Palette**: single accent = brand color.
- **Tempo**: 4s beats, tidy motion. **Music**: optimistic minimal electronic,
  105–115bpm.
- **Metaphors**: scattered documents aligning into a stack (order from
  chaos), tangled line untangling into a straight path (workflow), blocks
  slotting into a timeline (planning), many small panels synchronizing.

---

## Composing rules (apply to every archetype)

1. The base restraint anchor's NEGATIVES survive every adaptation (no text,
   no logos*, no god rays, no clutter, no people) — archetype ⑦'s two marked
   exceptions aside. *Logo-reminiscent arrangements in archetype ② are built
   from your own described shapes, never by asking for "the logo".
2. Temperament words modulate texture/lighting; palette policy sets colors;
   tempo sets durations + motion adjectives + bpm. Change only these three
   groups; keep composition rules (upper two-thirds, text zone) intact.
3. When the screenshot contradicts the archetype default, the screenshot
   wins — recipes are priors, the brand's real site is evidence.
